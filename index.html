Banana ice cream

list of ingredients

  • 2 bananas
  • a few spoons of Yoghut
  • Chocolate Chips

list of steps, to Turn these ingredients into a tasty dish

  1. Slice the bananas
  2. Place the sliced bananas in a freezeer bag
  3. Put the bag in the freezer
  4. after 3 hours, take them out of the freezer
  5. put the frozen banana sliced in a blender
  6. add the Yoghut
  7. add the Chocolate chips
  8. blitz it !
